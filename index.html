How to play

  1. Press Start — the target card flips face up and the timer starts.
  2. Rearrange the blocks so the board matches the card exactly.
  3. Tap a column to pick up its top block, then tap another column to drop it. Tap again to deselect.
  4. The dashed column is a buffer for temporary moves — it must be empty when you finish.
  5. In Hard and Insane every block has a number: both color and number must match.
  6. Be fast and plan ahead — fewer seconds and fewer moves mean more points.
